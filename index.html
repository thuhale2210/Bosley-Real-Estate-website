Rob Sansone

Realtor/CEO of Bosley Real Estate

Before starting my career in Real Estate, I spent 20+ years in project management for a large IT firm. That experience taught me some very clear objectives, namely; understand the customer's need, provide it, deliver it within budget, and achieve the highest standards of customer satisfaction. These skills continue to serve me and my clients well. My goal is to provide valuable insight, information and experience. Whatever you are looking for, be it a starter home, an investment property or commercial space, I appreciate the challenges people face when looking for property and will do what it takes to help you find what you want. I love a challenge and will go the extra mile every time. They say that Toronto is the 4th best city in the world to live in. I disagree. As a lifetime resident of this city, I would argue that there is no better place to live.
